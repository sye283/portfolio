Klean Kart

Klean Kart is a carbon foodprint (carbon emissions from food) tracking app, helping users better understand the environmental impacts of their food choices and make more sustainable purchases.

The app leverages Android's Mobile Vision API to scan barcodes of food items in grocery stores. The app subsequently identifies the food item and returns its associated carbon footprint. Users can also search for similar food items and compare footprints to make more economic choices without sacrifing their particular diet. By monitoring their food purchases, users can track how their carbon footprint has changed over time.

Bridge Travel

Bridge Travel is a social travel planning platform. Friends create groups where they can compile, coordinate, and collaborate on all travel details (flights, hotels, activities, etc.) on one convenient platform.

Current travel planning tools make it difficult to plan group trips. People need to coordinate flights, accomodations, activities, itineraries, and everything else across scattered conversations and emails. It's also hard to keep each other accountable for booking. Bridge Travel is a social platform that solves this problem. Plan conveniently with tools such as reminders, to-do lists, ticket compilations, group messages, and direct flight/hotel bookings.
EthiCAL Social Good Team

EthiCAL is a student organization at UC Berkeley that designs and sells their own line of apparel. The organization fights against fast fashion and sources materials from sustainable suppliers. EthiCAL donates all profits from sales to local non-profits that support causes affected by fast fashion (labor rights, climate change, etc.)

This is my team from when I was in EthiCAL. As the 'Social Good' team, we sourced partnerships with non-profits and focused on promoting/furthering the sustainability & social mission of the organization.
