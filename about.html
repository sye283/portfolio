Get to know me!

Hey, I'm Sharon! A Bay Area native, I just graduated from UC Berkeley with a dual degree in Computer Sciece and Business Administration. I now work as a quantitative analysis analyst in Manhattan.

I am a huge traveler and hope to visit as many places as possible before I die. Some of my favorite countries have been Czech Republic, Croatia, and Korea. Right now, the top destinations on my bucket list are Johannesburg, Bolivia, and The Maldives. Besides traveling, I also love trying new foods and hopping around cafes. I have a Master Food List where I keep track of all the food and dessert places around the world I want to try. Some fun facts about me, I have a collection of several hundred differently designed wooden pencils from childhood and I cried the first time I tried the spicy ramen challenge (it's one of my favorite snacks now).

My admiration for art started in high school, when I fell in love with the idea of expressing a person's personality and passions through portraiture - through the textures of the brush, the colors, the facial expressions, the eyes. I am new to the world of digital design, but I similarly admire the way a good design and branding can convey values that words cannot. If you would like to work with me and have any design requests, I'd love to connect! Feel free to shoot me an email if you just want to chat as well :)
