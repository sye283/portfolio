Get to know me!

Hey! I'm Sharon - a quirky yet genuine individual with an admiration for design and a passion for problem-solving. I am currently working as a product manager for a mission-oriented crypto company.

If I were to pick two words that resonate with me, they would be adventure and efficiency. I love traveling, hiking, and all hands-on activities in general. I am always looking to try new hobbies and activities, and can often be found on airline websites searching for great flight deals. Right now, some top destinations on my bucket list are Johannesburg, Bolivia, and Sydney. When I'm not searching for flights, you may find me on one of several productivity apps that I use regularly. I value and adore efficiency, and have accumulated an extensive portfolio of productivity apps to keep track of my life. I am a proud jaywalker and an avid Excel-shortcut user who really appreciates all the small things that just make life more efficient :)

I am also a naturally curious person with many interests. One of my current interests is language learning. I am actively trying to learn Korean while working on improving my Cantonese and French. I actually recently spent an entire summer in France trying to improve my French (though beyond ordering croissants at bakeries, I'm not sure how successful the mission was). I also love art and design. My admiration for art started in high school when I became captivated by the idea of expressing a person's personality through portraiture. I similarly admire the way a good design and branding can convey values that words cannot, and self-taught myself digital design in college. I built this website to showcase some interesting works I've done. If you'd like to chat, please reach out!
