Get to know me!

Hey, I'm Sharon! A Bay Area native, I just graduated from UC Berkeley with a dual degree in Computer Sciece and Business Administration. I am now situated in Manhattan where I work as a quantitative analyst.

I am a huge traveler and hope to visit as many places as possible before I die. Some of my favorite countries have been Czech Republic, Croatia, and Korea. Right now, the top destinations on my bucket list are Johannesburg, Bolivia, and The Maldives. Besides traveling, I also love trying new foods and hopping around cafes. I have a Master Food List where I keep track of all the food and dessert places around the world I want to try. Some fun facts about me, I have a collection of several hundred differently designed wooden pencils from childhood and I cried the first time I tried the spicy ramen challenge (it's one of my favorite snacks now).

I've always been passionate about art and design. I think it's beautiful how much a design can say and how much impact and meaning it can carry. I especially love drawing people, but I am always looking for a new challenge. If you would like to work with me and have any design requests, I'd love to connect! Feel free to shoot me an email if you just want to chat as well - if you want tips or advice on navigating through college, living in the Bay Area, or anything else, I'd love to help in any way :)
